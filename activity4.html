Exciting Upgrades and New Functionalities of iPhone 16

Key Features

A18 Chip

Powered by the new A18 chip, enhancing processing capabilities and energy efficiency.

Camera Design

The rear camera system features a new stacked arrangement for the main and ultra-wide cameras, which aims to facilitate spatial video capture.

Improved RAM

The device comes with 8GB of RAM, which aids in multitasking and future-proofing against software updates.

Action Button

The iPhone 16 introduces a customizable Action Button, replacing the traditional mute switch.
